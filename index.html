SERVICES

On-Page Optimization

Imagine your website as a storefront on a bustling street. On-page optimization is like dressing your window display to attract customers. I meticulously fine-tune the details on your website, making it crystal clear to search engines what your content is all about and why it matters.

Title tags, meta descriptions, header tags – they all become powerful tools in my arsenal to transform your website into a magnet for search engines. Through strategic on-page optimization, I boost your website's ranking for your target keywords.

The result? A surge in organic traffic and a user experience that's smooth sailing for your visitors. They'll find what they're looking for quickly and easily, leading to increased engagement and conversions.

Services Process

  • Conduct a thorough on-page SEO checkup to identify areas for improvement.
  • Optimize important elements such as title tags, meta descriptions, and header tags with your business target keywords for maximum impact.
  • Build a strong internal linking structure, ensuring a user-friendly experience and making it easier for search engines to crawl your site.
  • Optimize image alt text with relevant keywords to help your images rank higher in search results.
  • Guarantee your website is mobile-friendly and loads lightning-fast for an amazing user experience.

The more high-quality links you have, the higher your website climbs in search rankings, leading to more organic traffic and brand awareness.

Services Process

UPES

UPES, a premier Indian university, has carved a distinct niche in higher education. Renowned for its focus on specialized fields, UPES offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in advanced engineering, computer science, law, business, and more fields. Established with a vision to bridge the industry-academia gap, UPES emphasizes practical learning through industry collaborations and a strong research orientation. Located in Dehradun, the university boasts a modern campus equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, fostering a vibrant learning environment for students seeking a career-oriented education.

Project Description

I spearheaded the in-house SEO team for UPES University, a prestigious institution seeking to boost its online presence and attract qualified students. Leveraging my expertise in strategic SEO planning and execution, I led a comprehensive analysis that included competitor research, SERP analysis, and content evaluation.

This data-driven approach identified critical gaps and shortcomings in UPES's website SEO strategy, particularly the lack of well-structured category pages. Based on these insights, I recommended the creation of targeted category pages to improve website architecture and keyword targeting.

APPROACH

The cornerstone of my strategy involved conducting a thorough keyword research analysis to identify search terms most relevant to prospective students. This allowed us to optimize website content and meta descriptions to target these key phrases, ensuring UPES appeared at the top of search results for programs students were actively researching. In tandem, the creation of category pages, a key recommendation arising from the initial analysis, improved website architecture and provided a dedicated landing point for targeted keywords. This significantly boosted UPES's ranking and visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs).

To further enhance user experience, I oversaw website optimizations for mobile-friendliness and fast loading speeds. This ensured a seamless user journey for potential students exploring UPES's academic offerings.

Through this comprehensive approach, I empowered UPES to achieve significant improvements in its online visibility.
